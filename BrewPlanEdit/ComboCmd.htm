Combined Filter Directive

The #Count / #Interval / #Binning / #Filter directives are treated as a special case. They are combined into a single "directive" so they are always in sync with the same number of entries. The directives are written to the plan as individual lines.

When reading the plan the individual directives may be in any order; however, the written plan will always have them in the same order.

By default, the combined directive has all 4 directives (count, interval, binning, filter). Toggling the checkbox to the left of the individual lines will remove that particular line.

For example, in the beginning of the plan you establish the 4 directives. Later you change the filters and interval, but leave the count and binning the same.

#count 10,12
#interval 120,120
#binning 2,2
#filter Red, Blue

... various targets, etc

#interval 240,240
#filter HAlpha, Oiii

As a result of this combination there are no comments allowed on the individual lines (notice that the Note field does not appear).

The combined directive can be converted into comments, then later uncommented.

The filter names are loaded from the Preferences dialog. You can select one of three filter sets available.
